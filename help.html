Step by step to get your own app password


1. Access this link to login in your gmail account

2. Click in your user icon in right-top of the screen and click Manage your Google Account

3. In the left of the screen you will see Security, click that

4. In the painel Signing in to Google click in App passwords(password required to enter the following page)

5. Click Select app, choose other and type in the label the text: "apppassword". After that, click in generate

6. It's done, the following letters are your password app!
